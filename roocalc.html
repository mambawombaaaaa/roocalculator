How It Works:

The jackpot is won when there are 3 greens in a row. All 3 green bets must hit during the rounds to win the jackpot.

Here's a breakdown:

  • Your bet per round is multiplied by 14 when a green wins.
  • The jackpot pool is split evenly across 3 rounds.
  • Your payout depends on how many other players bet on green during those rounds.

Important: The jackpot only triggers if there are 3 greens in a row!
